To add a user to your group, enter their Spotify profile link or user ID into the form located near the top of the page.

How to find a user's profile link or user ID
  • Navigate to their profile page in the Spotify desktop or mobile app
  • Click or tap the  · · ·  icon (located in the top right corner on mobile or to the right of the follow button in the desktop app)
  • Mouse over or tap Share in the dropdown menu
  • Click or tap Copy Link on mobile or Copy Profile Link in the desktop app to copy their profile link to your clipboard
  • If you're using the desktop app, you can click Copy Spotify URI under the Share dropdown menu; the user's ID can be found in their Spotify URI
    • e.g. spotify:user:MyUserID
Alternatively, users who have signed up with a custom user ID can be added by simply entering this ID into the form.
When you've finished adding users to your group, click the midl button to generate your group's personalized playlist!
